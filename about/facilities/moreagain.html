Seminar Session

A seminar session or workshop is an educational event designed to provide in-depth knowledge, hands-on learning, and professional development opportunities in a specific field or topic. These events are often hosted by museums, galleries, or educational institutions and can cater to a wide range of audiences, from students and professionals to artists and art enthusiasts.

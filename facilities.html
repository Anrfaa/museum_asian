Rental Services

The Museum of Asian Art provides equipment rental services for events, offering various cultural items such as Gamelan sets, artifacts, and blue boards. This service is designed to support organizations, schools, cultural groups, and event organizers who wish to incorporate traditional or cultural elements into their programs, performances, or exhibitions.
